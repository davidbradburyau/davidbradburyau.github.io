Outcome

The outcome if this project is successful will be in the form of a business that will be able to provide a solution for technology needs for businesses around Australia and possibly internationally. This would in turn streamline and simplify technology management for customers leaving them to invest less resources and time into it. The problem of having many operators having to combine to resolve a subpar result without maximising capabilities of an interconnected system will be solved by a single wholistic service provider who can deliver a more usable and creativity nurturing technology solution. The impact of this project will be increased productivity and a better technology experience for the customers who use the product.
